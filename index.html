Alternatively, some people thrive in more dynamic spaces, like cafes or co-working areas, where a bit of background noise can enhance creativity. Having a designated study area also promotes a mental association between the space and learning, improving the ability to focus. Experimenting with different environments can help you find the ideal setting to boost your motivation and retain information more effectively.
